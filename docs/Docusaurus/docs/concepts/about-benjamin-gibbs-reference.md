# About Benjamin Gibbs

## Overview

Benjamin Gibbs is the creator of Mississippi, and a principal-level software engineer and engineering leader with more than two decades of experience building scalable platforms, distributed systems, and high-performing engineering teams.

Benjamin has worked across S&P 500 enterprise environments and startups, with a focus on turning complex business problems into simple, reliable systems that deliver real business value.

Across both hands-on and leadership roles, Benjamin has modernized platforms, improved software quality, strengthened developer experience, and helped teams ship with greater speed, confidence, and consistency.
